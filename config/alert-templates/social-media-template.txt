SOCIAL MEDIA ALERT TEMPLATE
============================
Platform: Twitter/X, Facebook, NextDoor, Instagram

Guidelines:
- Lead with ALERT type and emoji (standard: ⚠️ watch, 🔴 warning, 🚨 emergency)
- Short sentences. High urgency. Accessible language.
- Hashtags: #[EventType] #[JurisdictionName] #[DisasterType]
- Include official info link and phone number
- Pin to top of official account pages
- Do NOT include speculation — only confirmed facts
